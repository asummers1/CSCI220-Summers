This page concerns various common cat breeds and styles.

Bombay Cat

These cats are known for their all-black coat. They typically have copper or green eyes.

Tabby

These cats are known for the characteristic 'M' pattern on their forehead. They have phaeomelananin, a type of melanin that gives them their orange color.

Bicolor cat

These cats have white fur along with fur of another color.

Calico cat

Calico cats represent domestic cats that have a tri-color coat. Unlike bicolor cats, they can have any three colors in their pattern -- there doesn't have to be white. They are almost exclusively female.
